Research Overview

My research explores the intersection of service operations, fairness, and AI-powered decision-making. I develop theoretical and data-driven models for service systems, with applications in healthcare, digital platforms, and public services.

Selected Themes

  • Fairness in Queues: Designing equitable service disciplines using optimization and game theory.
  • Customer Interaction with AI: Modeling user behavior in response to LLM-based interfaces and decision-support systems.
  • Learning and Scheduling: Integrating statistical learning into healthcare appointment scheduling and capacity planning.
